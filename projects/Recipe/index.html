Delicious Chicken Wings Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken wings
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together soy sauce, honey, olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, black pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using).
  3. Add the chicken wings to the bowl and toss to coat evenly. Let marinate for 15–30 minutes.
  4. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil or parchment paper and place a wire rack on top.
  5. Arrange the wings on the rack in a single layer.
  6. Bake for 25–30 minutes, flipping the wings halfway through, until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
